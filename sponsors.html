Sponsors


At Team Arcis, our pursuit of excellence in aeromodelling and UAV technology is made possible by the generous support of our sponsors. These industry leaders, driven by innovation and engineering excellence, provide us with the opportunity to design, build, and compete globally. Their contributions empower us to push the boundaries of aerospace engineering, allowing our team to proudly represent Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering and India at some of the world's most prestigious international aero design competitions. Their support fuels our ability to develop cutting-edge aircraft, excel in international competitions, and gain invaluable hands-on experience in aerospace engineering. By partnering with us, sponsors go beyond supporting a student club—they are investing in the future of aviation, technology, and engineering. Their involvement directly impacts the next generation of engineers, aerospace enthusiasts who will shape the future of aeronautics and UAV technology. Join us on this journey! Together, we can push the boundaries of innovation and build a future driven by excellence, creativity, and engineering expertise.
