2024-2025


The 2024-2025 competition year was a year with many firsts for the team. This year we only participated in AIAA DBF due to the smaller size of the team. Due to an interesting rulebook with focus on subsystems the team was pushed to learn and implement autonomous systems for landing the X-1 test vehicle along with designing and testing pylons with modular efficasy in mind.


Cabinet Members

Flight in the US!

The members of the team conducted a first of its workshop on basics of RC Aircrafts and Aero Design in the college for the students. The workshop comprised of concepts and theries and their practical applications culminating into a bespoke airplane designed to adhear and perform within the rules of a given competition. This workshop was a success with the total attendence being greater than 150 students.
